Language Quest!

Welcome brave adventurer!

You are weary from your travels. You join a hooded figure by a fire for a short rest. After you've regained your strength, the mysterious man offers you a quest.

"Aaaahh, you're awake! You seem fit enough for a quest! Hmmm... lets see.. lets see.. where is it.. where is it.. Ah! here it is." The wizard sorts through his pack and produces a blue vial that seems to be glowing. "Courageous adventurer, if you answer these questions I will give you this vial. Hmmmm yes. It will grant you with the knowledge of one of three languages. Only one! Yesss. A powerful language it is. Very powerful. However, if you fail to answer the questions you will meet your DOOM!" The wizard places the vial in his sleeve and raises his staff. " You may begin!"
